# things that we are interested in from metcheck response

## required info from metcheck

+ temperature - degrees celsius
+ chanceofrain - percentage
+ windgustspeed - miles per hour
+ humidity - percent
+ rain - mm per hour
+ dayOfWeek - 1 (sunday) to 7 (saturday)
